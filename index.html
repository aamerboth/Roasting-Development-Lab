How to roast well

Title description, Sep 2, 2017
Image

This is my reason for making this website. How to achieve a great roast that is as delicious as one you would get from a highly rated specialty roaster.

In my own journey I have read a lot of advice on how to roast well. I usually turn around and try to replicate this and end up with a roast that is decent but not spectacular. That is what I want to be able to roast, spectacular coffee. So here is what I know so far about this. This website is essentially my journey in home coffee roasting and I will be updating it as I learn more.

I love light roasted coffee with fruity notes. This starts with high quality beans usually from the horn of Africa but I also get some from central and south America.
